My Music Page

Welcome

Welcome to my music page. I love music in general, but especially I love four artists. They are Maneskin, Guns and Roses, Taylor Swift, and Pinguini Tattici Nucleari. I am sure you know them all, but just in case, the latter group is an Italian indie band.

My Heroes Photo

Maneskin

Maneskin is an Italian rock band formed in 2016. They gained international recognition after winning the Eurovision Song Contest in 2021 with their song "Zitti e buoni." The band's music is known for its energetic rock sound and charismatic performances.

Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift is a renowned American singer-songwriter known for her versatile music career. She has released numerous hit albums, including "Fearless," "1989," and "Lover." Taylor Swift's music spans genres from country to pop.

Guns N' Roses

Guns N' Roses is an iconic American rock band formed in 1985. They are famous for their legendary albums like "Appetite for Destruction" and hit songs like "Sweet Child o' Mine." The band's music is characterized by a blend of rock and roll and hard rock.

Pinguini Tattici Nucleari

Pinguini Tattici Nucleari is an Italian indie rock band known for their unique and poetic lyrics. Their music combines elements of alternative rock and pop. The band has gained a dedicated following for their emotionally charged songs.
